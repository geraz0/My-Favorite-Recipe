The French Toast Formula: A Simple yet Scrumptious Recipe

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/4 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, melted
  • Cinnamon or nutmeg for sprinkling (optional)
  • Your choice of syrup, fruit, nuts, and whipped cream for your toppings.
