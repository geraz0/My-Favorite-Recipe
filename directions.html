Toast to Perfection: A Step-by-Step French Toast Adventure


French Toast Recipe


Cooking Directions:

  1. Start by cracking the eggs into a shallow dish. Whisk them together with a fork until they're well beaten.
  2. Add the milk, sugar, and salt to the eggs. Whisk until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture is smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat. Grease it with a small amount of butter or cooking spray.
  4. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture. Coat both sides evenly, making sure to get the bread nice and wet.
  5. Place the bread slices on the skillet or griddle. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until golden brown.
  6. Repeat with the remaining bread slices.
  7. Serve the French toast warm. Sprinkle with cinnamon or nutmeg if desired.
  8. Enjoy with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, or whipped cream.
